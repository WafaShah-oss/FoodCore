Biryani REcipe

Chickhen Biryani

Ingredients

  • 500g basmati rice
  • 500g Chickhen(cut into pieces)
  • 1 Cup yogurt
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • Whole spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder

Directions:

  1. Boil rice till 70% cooked and set aside.
  2. Fry onions until golden brown and remove half for garnishing.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste to remaining onions and sauté.
  4. Add chicken, tomatoes, spices, and cook until chicken is tender.
  5. Layer rice and chicken mixture in a pot, top with fried onions
  6. Cover and cook (dum) on low flame for 15-20 minutes. Serve hot

Soup REcipe

Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 500g basmati rice
  • 500g Chickhen(cut into pieces)
  • 1 Cup yogurt
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • Whole spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Oil or ghee

Directions:

  1. Heat oil or butter in a pan and sauté onions and garlic until soft.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they break down.
  3. Pour in the broth and bring to a boil. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Blend the mixture until smooth, then strain if needed.
  5. Return to heat, season with salt, pepper, basil, and sugar.
  6. Add cream or milk if using, and stir well.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with croutons or herbs.

Soup REcipe

Chickhen Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the chopped onion, garlic, carrots, and celery in a little bit of oil until tender.
  2. Add the chicken and chicken broth to the pot.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the chicken is cooked through.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Pizza REcipe

Spicy Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g. pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Make the dough by combining warm water, yeast, flour, salt, and olive oil.
  3. Roll out the dough and spread pizza sauce over it.
  4. Add shredded mozzarella cheese and your desired toppings.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until crust is golden brown.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Salad REcipe

Italian Salad

Ingredients

  • 4-6 cups mixed greens (lettuce, arugula, spinach)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup sliced red onion
  • 1 cup sliced salami
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup crumbled mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Italian dressing, to taste

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, sliced red onion, salami, pepperoni, and mozzarella cheese.
  2. Toss gently to combine.
  3. Sprinkle chopped parsley over the top.
  4. Drizzle with Italian dressing to taste.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Mexican Food Recipe

Qninoa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed and drained
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: herbs, spices, or vegetables of your choice

Directions:

  1. Rinse quinoa in a fine mesh strainer and drain well.
  2. In a medium saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add diced onion and cook until translucent.
  4. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add quinoa and water or broth to the saucepan.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until quinoa is tender.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
